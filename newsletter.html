Putin Orders Partial Ceasefire, Trump Hopes For More

Russian President Putin ordered a 30-day ceasefire on Ukraine's energy infrastructure after a call with President Trump, but did not extend it to frontlines or civilian populations. Assault Allegation Rocks Antarctic Research Base

A member of a South African research team at a remote Antarctic base has been accused of physical assault and sexual harassment. The South African government is investigating and providing support to the team. The alleged perpetrator has apologized and is cooperating.

Israel Intercepts Houthi Missile After Ceasefire Collapse

The Israeli military intercepted a ballistic missile launched by Iran-backed Houthis in Yemen on Tuesday. The incident followed the collapse of a ceasefire between Israel and Hamas. Israeli airstrikes in Gaza have reportedly killed Hamas' head of government, among others.

BYD Claims New EV Charging System as Fast as Filling a Gas Tank

Chinese EV maker BYD claims its new charging system, the "super e-platform," will recharge vehicles in the time it takes to fill a gas tank, achieving 1,000 kW charging speeds. This would significantly outpace Tesla's Superchargers. The technology will initially be rolled out in two BYD models.

NVIDIA Unveils New AI Tool for Learning American Sign Language

NVIDIA has launched a new AI-powered free tool, named Signs, to make learning American Sign Language more accessible. The software uses AI to analyze learners movements and provides feedback.

Ecuador's President Calls for International Military Aid to Combat Gangs

Ecuador's President Daniel Noboa has called for US, European, and Brazilian armies to assist in his "war" against criminal gangs. He also wants the US to designate Ecuadorean gangs as terrorist groups. Noboa emphasizes the international nature of the drug trafficking problem.

India Launches Massive Floating Solar Power Plant

India has launched its largest floating solar park, the Omkareshwar Floating Solar Park, in Madhya Pradesh. The project showcases innovative engineering and is expected to generate substantial clean energy while conserving water.
