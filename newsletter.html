Economy, immigration, Elon Musk take center stage in German election

Germany's upcoming election sees the conservative CDU, led by Friedrich Merz, poised to oust Chancellor Olaf Scholz. The AfD is expected to significantly increase its vote share, though Merz has stated he will not form a coalition with them. Nigel Farage Predicts 'Political Revolution' in Europe

Nigel Farage, leader of the UK's Reform UK party, believes a "political revolution" similar to the one that saw Donald Trump's election in the US will spread across Europe. Farage argues that a growing anti-establishment sentiment is boosting support for parties like Reform UK, which he claims is gaining significant traction in the polls.

Hamas Releases Hostages in Exchange for Palestinian Prisoners

Hamas released five Israeli hostages as part of a ceasefire agreement with Israel, in exchange for over 600 Palestinian prisoners. The fragile ceasefire deal continues despite recent controversies surrounding the return of hostage remains.
